3rd FAF Team Lead meeting,
February 23rd 2025, 15:30

Present:
    Balthazar, Giebmasse, GreenKiller, Fichom (ML), Speed, Strydxr
Substitutes:
    Strydxr (Balance, Tournament), Fichom (Game)
Absent:
    DevOps, Matchmaker and FAFLive representative

Short notes (in order of presentation):

  • general: Fichom reminded it's fine if you have nothing to report on
  • Promotions: Other teams should notify and ask for help with communicating to community when there's an issue that they can't waste time on while trying to resolve problems (ping Fichom/promo team). Ideally teams should communicate when big changes are happening so we can preventatively announce it to community (game patches, balance patches, etc.). Team avatars for members and soon for leads. Different "event" mods (easter, Christmas, april 1st, etc.), maybe keep regular events for a few days, and April 1st event for two days max.
  • Balance: Balance patch was released, feedback was good. They are still working hard on the sACU rework.
  • Tournament: had a large meeting, details of which can be found at (https://docs.google.com/document/d/18p9_S6t1H3xZWGfi2baLU03IhnEYaVTrvZ4PUm7VfiQ/edit?tab=t.0). Introduction of Senior TD, which help with the calendar, administration, etc. Plans to review blitz tournaments and collab with matchmaker team for “tournament map week" in the matchmaker.
  • Trainer: Updating the wiki pages (archiving irrelevant guides, organizing existing, writing new ones). Plans to make map-specific guides.
  • Creative: Nothing of particular to report. Suggested a few ideas for events Fichom mentioned. Balthazar has a long list of “troll" mods that would be a good fit for April 1st. Fichom added that, if multiple ideas seem cool, we could split it over the Matchmaker (so one idea for 1v1, another for 2v2, etc.).
  • Moderation: They are onboarding two new members, have other projects they can't publicize.
  • Campaign: Team is very inactive, many old developers and maintainers are either inactive or left. They have instances of people banned from the main discord server trying to hop on to their dedicated server. Currently there are not enough members to really develop or maintain missions properly. They also need urgent help with updating and uploading missions - either a dedicated member, or Fichom suggests: exploring the option of having some sort of auto-update that would make it easier for them long term. Speed also participated, and agreed with what was said, had nothing else to add.
  • Game: Notes were sent by BlackYps during the meeting and read to others by Fichom: In the last patch (3818) we included some new textures. However, this brought the file size of env.nx2 where they are stored to over 512MB, so it wasn't cached by cloudflare anymore. Our server couldn't provide the big file to so many people that requested the file at the same time, so the download kept failing for mostly everybody. I released a hotfix that excluded the textures, so everything works now, but we still have to come up with a plan how we can ship the new textures. Other than that the patch seems stable, although it's hard to say with the amount of ddos going on.
