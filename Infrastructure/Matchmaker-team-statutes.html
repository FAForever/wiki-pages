Policies

Every team in the FAF community is required to have a mission statement, which became mandatory at the general meeting on the 6th of March, 2022, when the Governance structure proposal was accepted. The mission statement for the Matchmaker Team describes:

  • The roles of the team
  • The responsibilities of each role
  • How the team operates

All Matchmaker Team members are expected to act in accordance with the Code of Conduct of our community. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Being open
  • Being empathetic, welcoming, friendly, and patient
  • Being collaborative
  • Being inquisitive
  • Choosing words carefully
  • Being concise
  • Stepping down considerately

Roles

If you’re interested in contributing, please introduce yourself on the official Discord server in the #matchmaker-public channel.

Curator

Curators are responsible for regularly updating and maintaining the matchmaker map pools for a specific queue (1v1, 2v2, and 4v4). They ensure that the maps selected are exciting and accessible for players of all skill levels, that pools overall do not heavily favor one faction, and that pools appeal to a wide range of players with various map preferences. Curators work with other team members to produce pools, gather community feedback, and communicate with mapmakers regarding their submissions. [Guide for creating a matchmaker map pool.] (https://wiki.faforever.com/en/Infrastructure/matchmaker-pools-documentation)

Mapmakers

Mapmakers play a crucial role in the matchmaker team by creating and refining maps that enhance the gaming experience for players. They do so by designing engaging maps. incorporating feedback, providing map submissions, and collaborating with the team, in order to create maps that fit the team’s selection criteria and also align with community standards and preferences.

Data Analyst

Data Analysts gather and interpret data from the community and the API to help inform decisions regarding map pools and player preferences. They help the team determine what works and what doesn’t and should play a critical role in enhancing player engagement and ensuring the quality of the matchmaker experience.

Team Lead

The team lead acts as the liaison between the Matchmaker Team and the board of the association and/or other teams. The team lead provides leadership and direction, helping to set goals and priorities for the team’s activities, ensuring alignment with the overall objectives of the community. The team lead is elected by a vote among team members. 

Team Operations

The team primarily collaborates through Discord. Contributors are encouraged to stay engaged with other platforms, such as forums or community discussions, to maintain a connection with the wider community.

There are several Discord channels designated for the Matchmaker Team. These channels facilitate discussions and project management:

  • Matchmaker Public
  • Matchmaker Projects
  • Matchmaker Team

The general purpose of the Matchmaker Team is to enhance player engagement by curating and managing the matchmaker map pools effectively. This involves a continuous effort to support mapmakers and ensure that the matchmaker remains vibrant and inviting for all players.
