The SACU (or Support Armored Command Unit) is a T3 unit requiring its own factory (the Quantum Gate) to be built. Each non-Seraphim SACU has RAS (Resource Allocation System) and are preferred means of late-game resource generation. SACUs can be built using presets or “naked”, and, since upgrades don't get into reclaim, naked SACUs, upgraded later, are preferred for offense. All SACUs have omni.

 

UEF: Mass generation: 1; energy generation: 20; build power: 56

Right arm:

  • Heavy Plasma Refractor (800/30k): Area of effect damage and higher range
  • RAS (4500/90k): Adds +10 mass generation and +1000 energy generation

Back:

  • Personal Shield Generator (2k/100.2k): Adds 26k shield HP for -500 energy
  • Shield Generator Field (3.5k/360.8k): Replaces the personal shield with a projected shield with 52k HP for -1000 energy
  • Radar Jammer (300/8k) for -100 energy
  • CD2 Engineering Drone (380/8.7k): A flying engineer

Left arm:

  • Energy Accelerator (1k/44.7k): Fire rate is multiplied by 1.82
  • Enhanced Sensor System (600/20k): Greatly increases the range of on-board sensor systems

 

Aeon: Mass generation: 1; energy generation: 20; build power: 56

  • RAS (4500/90k): Adds +10 mass generation and +1000 energy generation

Back:

  • Nano-Repair System (1.5k/74k)
  • Personal Teleporter (15k/1500k)

Left arm:

  • Sacrificial System (150/2.5k): Allows to sacrifice the SACU
  • Rapid Fabricator (800/50.5k): Adds 42 build power

 

Cybran: Mass generation: 1; energy generation: 20; build power: 56

Left arm:
