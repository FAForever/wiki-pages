"Titan" is a UEF land unit included in *Forged Alliance Forever*.
It is classified as a tech 3 heavy assault bot unit.
The build description for this unit is:

<blockquote>Extremely fast Heavy Raiding Bot. Equipped with a quickly-regenerating Personal Shield, allowing it to easily recover from smaller engagements.</blockquote>
