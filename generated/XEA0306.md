"Continental" is a UEF aircraft unit included in *Forged Alliance Forever*.
It is classified as a tech 3 heavy air transport unit.
The build description for this unit is:

<blockquote>Heavily armoured, shielded Air Transport. Armed with two Plasma Cannons and four anti-air SAM Launchers. Can carry 28 T1 units, 12 T2 units (Plus 4 T1), or 6 T3 units (Plus 4 T1).</blockquote>
