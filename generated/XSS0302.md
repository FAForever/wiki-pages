"Hauthuum" is a Seraphim naval unit included in *Forged Alliance Forever*.
It is classified as a tech 3 battleship unit.
The build description for this unit is:

<blockquote>Long-range Surface Bombardment Ship. Armed with three direct fire cannons and two weak anti-air cannons. Equipped with two tactical missile defense systems. Can build and launch strategic nuclear weapons.</blockquote>
