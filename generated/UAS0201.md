"Exodus Class" is an Aeon naval unit included in *Forged Alliance Forever*.
It is classified as a tech 2 destroyer unit.
The build description for this unit is:

<blockquote>Primary surface combatant, armed with a powerful direct fire cannon, torpedoes and depth charges. Equipped with an effective torpedo defense.</blockquote>

Hover over abilities to see effect descriptions.
