"Sele-istle" is a Seraphim aircraft unit included in *Forged Alliance Forever*.
It is classified as a tech 1 air scout unit.
The build description for this unit is:

<blockquote>Fast aerial reconnaissance vehicle. If shot down, its wreck creates a field of vision for a brief moment.</blockquote>
