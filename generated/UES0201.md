"Valiant Class" is a UEF naval unit included in *Forged Alliance Forever*.
It is classified as a tech 2 destroyer unit.
The build description for this unit is:

<blockquote>Primary surface combatant, armed with powerful direct fire cannons and weak torpedoes. Equipped with an anti-air Railgun and torpedo defense.</blockquote>
