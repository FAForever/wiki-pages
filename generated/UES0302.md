"Summit Class" is a UEF naval unit included in *Forged Alliance Forever*.
It is classified as a tech 3 battleship unit.
The build description for this unit is:

<blockquote>Extremely long-range Surface Bombardment Ship. Armed with three direct fire cannons and weak anti-air Railguns. Equipped with two tactical missile defense systems.</blockquote>
