"Aluminar" is an Aeon aircraft unit included in *Forged Alliance Forever*.
It is classified as a tech 2 air transport unit.
The build description for this unit is:

<blockquote>Lightly armed Air Transport, equipped with anti-air weapons. Can carry 12 T1 units, 6 T2 units, or 3 T3 units.</blockquote>

### Transport capacity
This unit has 12 small attach points, 6 medium attach points, and 3 large attach points. Using an attach point costs a number of docking slots. Specifically 1 for small, 2 for medium, and 4 for large.
