"CZAR" is an Aeon aircraft unit included in *Forged Alliance Forever*.
It is classified as a experimental aircraft carrier unit.
The build description for this unit is:

<blockquote>Experimental Floating Fortress. Armed with unbelievably powerful central beam, incredible anti-air weaponry and depth charges. Equipped with air staging, a full sensor suite and an air factory.</blockquote>
