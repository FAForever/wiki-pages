"Broadsword" is a UEF aircraft unit included in *Forged Alliance Forever*.
It is classified as a tech 3 heavy gunship unit.
The build description for this unit is:

<blockquote>Heavily armoured Gunship, armed with a powerful Plasma Cannon and a weak anti-air Railgun. Equipped with a radar jammer. Costs energy to run.</blockquote>
