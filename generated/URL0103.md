"Medusa" is a Cybran land unit included in *Forged Alliance Forever*.
It is classified as a tech 1 mobile light artillery unit.
The build description for this unit is:

<blockquote>Front-loaded Mobile Artillery unit, armed with an EMP shell, capable of stunning T1 and T2 units. Lower overall damage output compared to its counterparts.</blockquote>
