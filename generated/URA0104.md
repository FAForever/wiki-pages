"Dragon Fly" is a Cybran aircraft unit included in *Forged Alliance Forever*.
It is classified as a tech 2 air transport unit.
The build description for this unit is:

<blockquote>Lightly armed Air Transport, equipped with anti-air lasers and a weak direct-fire weapon, capable of stunning enemy units. Can carry 10 T1 units, 4 T2 units (Plus 2 T1), or 2 T3 units (Plus 2 T1).</blockquote>
