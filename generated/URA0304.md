"Revenant" is a Cybran aircraft unit included in *Forged Alliance Forever*.
It is classified as a tech 3 strategic bomber unit.
The build description for this unit is:

<blockquote>Strategic Bomber, designed to surgically destroy key targets. Armed with light anti-air weaponry. Equipped with a personal stealth generator. Costs energy to run.</blockquote>
