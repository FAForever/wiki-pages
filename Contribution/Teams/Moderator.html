Policies:

Moderation Team Lead


• Acting as the final point of appeal for all moderation
decisions
• Determining the membership and structure of the
moderation team
• Chosen by the moderation team, with a 12 month
cooldown period during normal circumstances

 

Team Members


• Interested candidates send their application to the
team lead
• Applicants are discussed and voted for within the
team, with the team lead having the final say regarding
new team members
• Team members can be dropped from the team due to
long inactivity or being deemed no longer suitable for
the position
• Moderation team members can be split between
global moderators, discord moderators, IRC
moderators and forum moderators
